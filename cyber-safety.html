Stop Cyberbullying

What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is when someone uses technology to be mean to others. This includes:

  • Sending mean messages or emails
  • Posting hurtful comments
  • Sharing embarrassing photos without permission
  • Leaving someone out of online groups on purpose

What to Do if You See Cyberbullying

1

Don't Join In

Never participate in cyberbullying, even if others are doing it.

2

Don't Forward

Don't share or forward mean messages or embarrassing content.

3

Support the Person

Be kind to the person being bullied. Send them a nice message.

4

Tell an Adult

Report cyberbullying to a parent, teacher, or other trusted adult.
