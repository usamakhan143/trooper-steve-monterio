The Safety Rule

1

STOP

Stop what you're doing right away. Don't move closer.

2

DON'T TOUCH

Never touch a gun. It's not a toy and can be very dangerous.

3

RUN AWAY

Leave the area immediately. Get to a safe place.

4

TELL A GROWN-UP

Find a trusted adult and tell them what you saw right away.
