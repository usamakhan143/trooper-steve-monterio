Know Your Trusted Adults

These are the grown-ups who love you and keep you safe

Family Members

  • Parents and guardians
  • Grandparents
  • Aunts and uncles
  • Older siblings (with parents)

These are your closest family who love and care for you every day.

School Helpers

  • Teachers
  • School counselors
  • Principals
  • School nurses

Adults at school who are there to help you learn and stay safe.

Community Helpers

  • Police officers
  • Firefighters
  • Doctors and nurses
  • Librarians

Community workers whose job is to help and protect everyone.

Your Body Belongs to You

Private Parts are Private

Your private parts are the parts of your body covered by a bathing suit. These parts are special and private to you.

You Can Say "No"

If someone makes you feel uncomfortable or asks you to do something that doesn't feel right, you can always say "No" and tell a trusted adult.

Always Tell a Trusted Adult

If someone touches you in a way that makes you uncomfortable, or asks you to keep a secret that makes you feel bad, tell a trusted adult right away.

Good Touch vs Bad Touch

Good touches make you feel happy and safe, like hugs from family. Bad touches make you feel uncomfortable or scared.
