Know Your Swimming Level

ğŸ¤½â€â™€ï¸

Beginning Swimmer

You should:

  • Always wear a life jacket
  • Stay in shallow water
  • Have an adult within arm's reach
  • Practice floating and kicking
ï¿½ï¿½ï¿½â€â™‚ï¸

Learning Swimmer

You can:

  • Float on your back and front
  • Swim a short distance
  • Tread water for a few seconds
  • Jump in and get back to the wall
ğŸ†

Strong Swimmer

You can:

  • Swim continuously for 25 yards
  • Tread water for 1 minute
  • Dive safely in deep water
  • Help others (with adult guidance)

Remember: Even strong swimmers need to follow safety rules!

No matter how well you swim, always have adult supervision and follow pool rules.
